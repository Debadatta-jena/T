al# Security Policy for Trionex Technologies

## Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

**Email:** debadattajena552@gmail.com

We appreciate your help keeping our website and services secure. We will aim to acknowledge your report within 24-48 hours.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- Trionex Technologies website (trionex.tech)
- All web applications and services provided by Trionex Technologies

## What to Include in Your Report

Please include:
1. Description of the vulnerability
2. Steps to reproduce the issue
3. Potential impact of the vulnerability
4. Any suggested fixes (optional)

## What You Can Expect From Us

- Acknowledgment of your report within 24-48 hours
- Regular updates on our progress
- Credit in our security hall of fame (if desired)

## Out of Scope

The following are not eligible for bug bounties:
- Reports from third-party services
- Issues related to outdated browsers
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security issues
