- **Digital**: 24px height for readability

- **Mobile**: 32px minimum touch target

### Clear Space
- Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "AI" text
- No other elements should infringe on this space

### Color Variants

#### Colored Logo (Primary)
- **Icon**: Blue gradient (#2563eb to #9333ea)

- **Usage**: Most applications, primary branding

- **Icon**: Dark gray (#111827)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#111827)
- **Usage**: Light backgrounds, dark mode

#### Dark Logo
- **Icon**: White (#ffffff)
- **Text**: White (#ffffff)
- **Usage**: Dark backgrounds, overlays

## üö´ Logo Misuse

### Don't:
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the colors or proportions
- Add drop shadows or effects
- Place on busy backgrounds
- Rotate or flip the logo
- Use outdated versions

### Do:
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Use approved color variants
- Ensure sufficient contrast
- Follow clear space guidelines
- Use current logo files
